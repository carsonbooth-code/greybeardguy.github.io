Privacy Policy

Effective April 23, 2026 Last Updated April 23, 2026

Our Core Commitment

We do not collect, receive, transmit, store, or have any access to your personal data. Ever.

This is not a legal formality — it is the fundamental design principle of The App. All data you enter lives exclusively on your device. We have no servers, no databases, no accounts, and no infrastructure capable of receiving your information, nor will we ever, nor will we want to.

We will never pollute the app with advertising, nor collect any data for re-sell or advertising purposes. The App will always remain a passion project.

Definition — Throughout this privacy policy, "The App" refers to GetPhyt, an iOS fitness tracking application developed by Carson Booth under the GreyBeardGuy brand.
Data & Privacy
1
Who We Are

Data Controller (for GDPR purposes)
Carson Booth · Individual Developer — GreyBeardGuy · Berlin, Germany · support@greybeardguy.app

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), a data controller is the person or entity that determines how personal data is processed. Because The App processes all data exclusively on your device and we never receive any data, our role as data controller is limited entirely to the design decisions embedded in the application itself.

2
Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicit, The App does not collect, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers, IP address, or advertising IDs
  • Your Apple ID or iCloud account information
  • Your health or fitness data of any kind
  • Your activity logs, goals, or personal records
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or behavioral data
  • Any information whatsoever

There are no analytics SDKs, tracking libraries, advertising networks, or third-party frameworks embedded in The App that could collect data on our behalf.

The One Exception: Support Email

If you choose to contact us by email for support purposes, we will receive and temporarily retain the information contained in that email solely for the duration required to respond or address the request. This may include your name or email alias, your device or iOS version if you choose to include it, a description of the issue you are experiencing, and any enhancement or corrective action requested.

This information is used exclusively to respond to your support inquiry.
It is not stored in any database, shared with any third party, or used for any purpose beyond resolving your request. Support emails are subject to the privacy practices of Apple's iCloud Mail service and are retained only for as long as necessary to address your request, after which they are deleted. You may request earlier deletion of any support correspondence by contacting us at support@greybeardguy.app.

3
Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your iPhone using Apple's Core Data framework:

  • Activity types and personal fitness goal configurations
  • Activity log entries you create (counts, durations, dates, notes)
  • Health data imported from Apple HealthKit (steps, distance, weight, flights climbed)
  • App settings and preferences
  • We may continue to add or modify activity types, based on user feedback and great ideas, including more HealthKit activites.

This data never leaves your device through The App. It is stored in your device's local storage and is subject to your device's own security, encryption, and backup settings.

4
Apple HealthKit

The App can optionally connect to Apple HealthKit to import health and fitness data such as step counts, walking and running distance, cycling distance, swimming distance, body weight, and flights climbed.

Regarding HealthKit data:
  • The App requests read-only access. We never write data back to HealthKit.
  • HealthKit data is imported into your local App database on your device only.
  • We never access, transmit, or store your HealthKit data on any server.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in The App's Settings or in your iPhone's Health app under Sources.
  • Deleting HealthKit-imported data from The App does not affect your Apple Health data.

Apple's own HealthKit privacy terms apply to how Apple handles your health data within the Health app. The App's use of HealthKit complies with Apple's HealthKit developer guidelines, which prohibit using health data for advertising or selling to third parties.

5
iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your App data may be included in your iCloud backup as part of your overall device backup. This is controlled entirely by Apple and your device settings. The App does not initiate, manage, or have access to iCloud backups. Please refer to Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles backup data.

6
No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, platforms, or APIs beyond Apple's own frameworks (Core Data, HealthKit, and SwiftUI). There are no:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors
  • Crash reporting services
Your Rights
7
Your Rights Under GDPR (European Union Users)

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) grants EU residents specific rights regarding their personal data. Because The App does not collect or process your personal data on any server or system we control, most GDPR rights apply to data stored locally on your device, which is entirely under your control.

Right of Access

All your App data is stored on your device and is directly accessible to you within the app.

Right to Rectification

You can edit or correct any data within The App at any time.

Right to Erasure

You can delete individual activities, all activities of a type, or all data by deleting The App from your device.

Right to Data Portability

The App includes a built-in Archive & Email feature that allows you to export your activity data as a JSON file to any email address you choose.

Right to Object

Since we process no data on our end, there is nothing to object to. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your data rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Germany, this is the Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI) at www.bfdi.bund.de.

8
Your Rights Under US Privacy Laws

For users in the United States, including residents of California (CCPA/CPRA), Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), and other states with privacy legislation:

Because The App does not collect, sell, share, or process your personal information on any system we control, the following applies:

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not use your personal information for targeted advertising.
  • There is no personal information for us to disclose, delete on request, or correct on request, as it never reaches us.

California residents: The App does not meet the threshold for mandatory CCPA compliance (we are an individual developer with no commercial data activities), but we voluntarily commit to the same privacy standards.

Additional Information
9
Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from children under the age of 13 in the United States (COPPA) or under the age of 16 in the European Union (GDPR). Since The App does not collect any personal information from any user, there is no special risk to children's privacy. However, The App is designed for adult fitness tracking and is not directed at children.

The App includes a Mature Content setting which, when enabled, allows the tracking of certain adult activities (alcohol consumption, cycle tracking, sex, for example). This setting is disabled by default and must be explicitly enabled by the user. By enabling this setting, the user confirms they are of appropriate age to access such content in their jurisdiction. Parents and guardians are encouraged to review their device's Screen Time and app restriction settings to prevent minors from accessing inappropriate content within The App.

10
Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your App data depends on your device security settings. We strongly recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your iOS software up to date
  • Enabling iCloud Backup encryption if you use iCloud Backup
11
Data Retention

The App allows you to retain your data on your device for as long as the app is installed. You control your data retention entirely — you can delete individual entries, archive and remove old entries using the built-in Archive feature, or delete the app to remove all data permanently.

12
Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this document and publish the updated policy. Since The App is a privacy-first application with no data collection, significant changes to this policy are unlikely.
