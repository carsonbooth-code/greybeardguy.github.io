Why FitLogger
Your Data, Your Device
Everything stays on your iPhone. No accounts, no servers, no cloud — just your data, locally stored and privately kept.
Beyond Apple Fitness
Track push-ups, pull-ups, sit-ups, creatine, collagen, fasting, water and dozens more activities Apple Fitness ignores.
Rich Charts
Donut, bar, trend, timeline and weight charts across daily, weekly, monthly and yearly views — with goal tracking built in.
HealthKit Import
Pull in steps, distance, flights climbed, cycling, weight and more from Apple Health — read-only, device-only, always private.
Archive & Export
Archive old activities and email them as structured JSON. Your records, portable and yours to keep forever.
No Ads. Ever.
FitLogger is a passion project. No advertising, no data selling, no tracking. Just a clean tool built by a developer who uses it daily.
