About

Made by a greybard,
for greybeards.

I'm Carson — a 58-year-old indie developer based in Berlin. I built FitLogger because I couldn't find a fitness app that got out of the way and let me track what matters.

No subscriptions. No social feed. No AI-generated workout plans. Just clean, honest logging — the way it should be.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

01

Activity tracking

Log any workout with custom activity types. Built for the way real people train, not influencer circuits.

02

HealthKit sync

Pulls walking, running, and other data from Apple Health automatically. Your data, in one place.

03

Progress charts

Visual trends over any time range. See what's actually working — without the noise.

04

Goal tracking

Set targets per activity. Stay accountable across weeks and months, not just today.

05

Timezone aware

Travels with you. Workouts land in the right day no matter where in the world you train.

06

100% free

No subscriptions. No in-app purchases. No ads. Just the app.
